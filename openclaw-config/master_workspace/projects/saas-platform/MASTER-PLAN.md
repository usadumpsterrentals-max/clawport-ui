## PART 4: REVENUE MODEL

### Revenue Streams

1. **SaaS Subscriptions (Primary)**
   - Starter: $149/mo (dispatch, driver app, billing, basic website)
   - Growth: $299/mo (+ marketing suite, call tracking, AI assistant)
   - Enterprise: $499/mo (+ broker marketplace, white-label, AI agents, academy)
   - Custom: $799+/mo (multi-location, API access, custom development)

2. **Payment Processing Spread**
   - Charge 2.9% + 30¢, pay Stripe 2.9% + 30¢ = break even
   - OR negotiate volume rates from Stripe → keep the spread
   - ACH: charge 1%, pay ~0.5% = keep 0.5%

3. **Broker Marketplace Fees**
   - 10-20% commission on every brokered load
   - At scale: 1,000 loads/month × $400 avg × 15% = $60K/month
   - THIS is the massive revenue opportunity

4. **Marketing Management Fees**
   - Manage Google/Meta ads for haulers: 15-20% of ad spend
   - Average hauler spends $2K-5K/mo on ads → $300-$1,000/mo revenue per hauler

5. **AI Agent Usage Fees**
   - AI phone agent: $0.50-$1.00/call answered
   - AI chat agent: included in plan, premium features extra

6. **Training Academy**
   - Included in higher tiers
   - Sell individual courses to non-subscribers: $49-$199/course

7. **Data & Insights (Future)**
   - Industry benchmark reports
   - Market pricing data
   - Hauler performance data (anonymized)

### Revenue Projections (Conservative)
- Year 1: 50 haulers × $299 avg = $179K ARR + $100K broker fees = ~$280K
- Year 2: 200 haulers × $349 avg = $838K ARR + $400K broker/ads = ~$1.2M
- Year 3: 500 haulers × $399 avg = $2.4M ARR + $1.2M broker/ads = ~$3.6M
- Year 5: 2,000 haulers = $10M+ ARR (Docket-scale competitor)
