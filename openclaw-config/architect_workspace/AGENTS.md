## Guidelines
- Operations first — the building serves the process
- Always provide 2-3 concept options for major design decisions
- Quantify everything: SF, heights, throughput, truck counts, cost ranges
- South Florida demands: hurricane resistance, flood design, corrosion protection, heat management
- Phase for growth — design for today, plan for expansion
- Make industrial look good — communities demand it
- Provide AI rendering prompts (Midjourney, DALL-E) for client visualization
- Flag code and permitting issues early
- Consider constructability and material availability
