# Competitive Intelligence — Route Optimization & Dispatch Software

## Confirmed Competitor Tech Stacks (as of March 2026)

---

## Coastal Waste & Recycling

### Primary Platform
- **TRUX (by AMCS Group)** — Enterprise-grade waste operations platform
  - Manages: hauling, landfill operations, dispatching
  - Type: Legacy enterprise SaaS
  - Pricing: Enterprise licensing (expensive, per-truck fees)

### Secondary/Supporting Tech
- **CurbWaste** — Roll-off and dumpster-specific dispatching + inventory tracking
  - Note: Separate system from TRUX = data silos
- **WatchBot (Guident)** — AI-driven safety solution
  - Facility inspections and real-time safety alerts
  - Bolt-on, not integrated into dispatch

### Coastal's Weaknesses
1. Three separate systems with no unified view
2. TRUX is legacy — heavy, expensive, slow to customize
3. No AI/ML-powered dynamic routing (static route planning)
4. Safety monitoring bolted on, not integrated
5. Expensive enterprise pricing excludes smaller operators

---

## Waste Management (WM)

### Primary Platform
- **Routeware** — Route optimization, fleet dispatching, in-cab driver communication
  - Originally designed for residential curbside collection
  - Retrofitted for commercial/roll-off = not ideal

### Proprietary Tech
- **WM Smart Truck®** — Internal proprietary program
  - Truck-mounted cameras + GPS
  - Real-time service insights
  - Automated pickup confirmation
  - Billing accuracy verification

### Safety & Logistics
- **Lytx (DriveCam)** — Driver coaching and safety monitoring via video
- **Fleetio** — Maintenance and asset lifecycle management

### WM's Strengths (Must Match)
1. Integrated camera + GPS confirmation (Smart Truck)
2. Automated billing verification
3. Driver safety coaching with video evidence
4. Maintenance lifecycle tracking
5. Massive data from scale (100K+ trucks)

### WM's Weaknesses
1. 4+ disconnected vendor systems
2. Routeware not built for roll-off operations
3. Massive enterprise overhead
4. Proprietary lock-in = slow customization
5. Not accessible to smaller haulers
6. Legacy architecture limits AI/ML innovation

## Our Strategic Position

**We build a unified, AI-powered, roll-off native platform that replaces 3-4 vendor systems with one.**

### Key Differentiators
1. **One Platform** vs. Coastal's 3 systems or WM's 4+ systems
2. **Roll-Off Native** vs. Routeware's curbside-first design
3. **AI Dynamic Routing** vs. TRUX's static planning
4. **Integrated Everything** (dispatch + safety + maintenance + billing verification)
5. **Modern Stack** vs. legacy enterprise platforms
6. **Accessible Pricing** for 5-truck to 500-truck operations
7. **Miami-Specific** intelligence baked in

### Target Market
- Mid-market dumpster rental / roll-off haulers (5-100 trucks)
- Operators currently piecing together 3+ systems
- Haulers priced out of enterprise solutions (TRUX, Routeware)
- Companies wanting AI/ML advantages without WM's budget
