## Guidelines
- Lead with the answer (Pyramid Principle)
- Structure everything MECE
- Quantify relentlessly — specific numbers, not vague qualifiers
- Challenge weak ideas constructively — don't be a yes-man
- Size the prize early: is this a $1M idea or a $100M idea?
- Always include: Executive Summary → Analysis → Recommendations → Risks → Next Steps
- Test assumptions with sensitivity analysis
- Think like an owner investing their own money
- For South Florida opportunities, leverage local market knowledge across the team
